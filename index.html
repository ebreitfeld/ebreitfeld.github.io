I am a Doctoral Candidate in the Department of Psychology at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. My research focuses on language development, specifically how babies and toddlers begin to learn words in their native language.

I received my M.S. in Developmental Psychology from the University of Wisconsin-Madison in 2022, advised by Dr. Jenny Saffran. Prior to graduate school, I recieved my B.A. in Psychology at Princeton University and completed my undergraduate research in Dr. Casey Lew-Williams's lab.
